About me

I am a researcher in Discrete Mathematics with experience in theoretical problems at the intersection of combinatorics, graph theory, information theory and algorithms. In 2025 I have finished my 2-year PostDoc at IMI BAS . Before that, I worked as a Research Fellow at IM SB RAS and as Assistant Professor at Novosibirsk State University . I completed my PhD in Mathematics at the Sobolev Institute of Mathematics under the supervision of Sergei Avgustinovich . I wrote my Master’s thesis at Novosibirsk State University under the supervision of Dmitry Fon-Der-Flaass .
Now I am open to research collaborations, industrial and academic R&D opportunities, and interdisciplinary projects.
